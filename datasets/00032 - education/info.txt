Description: <p>This dataset contains the results of surveying students and professors in the Faculty of Informatics, <a href="http://cujae.edu.cu/">Instituto Superior Politecnico Jose Antonio Echeverria (Cujae, Havana, Cuba)</a> about their preferences on courses and the most important aspects affecting their performance as students and professionals. Answers include ties and missing elements. These surveys, conducted in 2015, include criteria about different numbers of aspects (6 to 32 candidates) and 13 courses.</p><p>This dataset was donated by <a href="https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Alejandro_Rosete-Suarez">Alejandro Rosete Suarez</a> and <a href="https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Milton_Garcia-Borroto">Milton Garcia Borroto</a> and may be augmented with new surveys in the future.</p>
